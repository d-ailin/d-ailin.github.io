Hi! I am a PhD student at National University of Singapore working towards trustworthy machine learning. I am fortuned to be advised by Bryan Hooi.

My research interests lie in understanding the underlying reasons behind the failures of deep neural networks and their relationship with data, with the ultimate goal of improving the reliability of these models to be deployed in the real-world.

Interested Topics: failure prediction, calibration, out-of-distribution, anomaly detection, fairness, representation learning, uncertainty quantification and generalization.
