Hi! I am a PhD student in Computer Science at National University of Singapore, advised by Bryan Hooi.

My research goal is to build systems in an efficient, interpretable and reliable way. I am also interested in understanding the inherent connections between different objects (e.g., model vs data, data vs data, etc).

Recent Interested Topics: failure prediction, calibration, out-of-distribution, anomaly detection, fairness, representation learning, uncertainty quantification, interpretability and generalization.

Feel free to reach out () if you are interested in my research or collaborate :)

Publications

Great Models Think Alike: Improving Model Reliability via Inter-Model Latent Agreement Ailin Deng, Miao Xiong, Bryan Hooi ICML 2023 TL;DR A prediction tend to be more reliable when its neighborhood in latent spaces is similar to those obtained from a foundation model.
Probabilistic Knowledge Distillation for Face Ensembles Jianqing Xu, Shen Li, Ailin Deng, Miao Xiong, Jiaying Wu, Jiaxiang Wu, Shouhong Ding, Bryan Hooi CVPR 2023 TL;DR A bayesian view of ensembles and its application in open-set (i.e. face recognition) problem.
Trust, but Verify: Using Self-Supervised Probing to Improve Trustworthiness Ailin Deng, Shen Li, Miao Xiong, Zhirui Chen, Bryan Hooi ECCV 2022 TL;DR / paper / code A prediction tend to be more reliable if it can also predict some related tasks accurately.
Birds of a Feather Trust Together: Knowing When to Trust a Classifier via Adaptive Neighborhood Aggregation Miao Xiong, Shen Li, Wenjie Feng, Ailin Deng, Jihai Zhang, Bryan Hooi TMLR 2022 TL;DR / paper / code Let's combine the original prediction and the neighborhood samples' predictions to make a decision.
CADET: Calibrated Anomaly Detection for Mitigating Hardness Bias Ailin Deng, Adam Goodge, Lang Yi Ang, Bryan Hooi IJCAI 2022 TL;DR / paper / code Existing methods tend to assign higher anomaly scores to the 'harder' samples.
Graph Neural Network-Based Anomaly Detection in Multivariate Time Series Ailin Deng, Bryan Hooi AAAI 2021 TL;DR / paper / code A model to detect time series anomalies with some abilities of localizing and understanding why an anomaly happen.
